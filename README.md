**Shayish Kfar Yassif** (ОЕОЎОЕ ОЏОцОе ОЎОљОАОЎОБ) is a premium marble and ceramic surfaces business based in Kfar Yassif, Israel. Since 2008, we've been crafting custom stone solutions for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial spaces.

- Custom marble countertops
- Ceramic tile installations
- Natural stone flooring
- Commercial and residential projects
